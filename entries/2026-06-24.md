**Austin Tedesco's 4-Step AI Writing System: Ideas Bank to Outline to Draft to Monologue**
Austin Tedesco (head of growth at Every) built a personal AI writing workflow for his food newsletter that uses AI as thought partner without outsourcing the prose. Step 1: Create a standing writing doc with three sections — Ideas Bank, Outline, Draft. Step 2: Send messy ideas via phone text to an agent connected to an AI writing assistant trained on his tone and his Substack archive; it organizes ideas, flags connections to past pieces, notes where his thinking has changed. Step 3: By the time he opens his laptop, the doc has a detailed outline; open in Codex and write the draft in one sitting.
