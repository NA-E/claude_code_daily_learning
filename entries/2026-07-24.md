Scores 57 on Artificial Analysis's Intelligence Index — third overall behind Claude Fable 5 (60) and GPT-5.6 Sol (59), ahead of GLM-5.2 (51). Priced at $3.00/$0.30/$15.00 per million input/cached/output tokens; completes the Intelligence Index at $0.95/task, cheaper than Fable 5's $2.75/task. Available now via Kimi.com, Kimi Code CLI, and API; open weights promised by July 27, which would make it the largest open-weights model released to date.
